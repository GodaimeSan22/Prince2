Risk

PURPOSE: UNCERTAINTY

Identifies, assesses, and controls uncertainty. Risks can be Threats (negative) or Opportunities (positive).

Exam Tip: Risk Appetite is the amount of risk an organization is willing to accept.

Issues

PURPOSE: CHANGE CONTROL

To identify, assess, and control any potential and actual changes to the baseline. Handles: Requests for Change, Off-specifications, and Problems.

Uses a Change Authority to approve or reject changes without involving the Board every time.

Progress

PURPOSE: CONTROL

"Where are we compared to where we planned to be?"

Uses Time-driven (Highlight) and Event-driven (End Stage) controls to monitor and compare actual achievements against those planned.
